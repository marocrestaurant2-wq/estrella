Privacy Policy (Mobile App)

This Privacy Policy applies to the ESTRELLA HOLDING mobile application published by ESTRELLA HOLDING SARL AU.

Types of Data Collected

Our mobile app collects only the personal information you voluntarily provide through the in-app contact form:

  • Name (required in the contact form)
  • Email address (required to respond to your inquiry)
  • Phone number (optional for follow-up communication)

No other personal, sensitive, or background data (such as location, camera, contacts, or files) is collected without your explicit consent.

Purpose of Collection

We collect this information solely to:

  • Enable the app’s contact functionality (App Functionality)
  • Communicate with you in response to your inquiries (Developer Communications)

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. The information you submit is transmitted securely to ESTRELLA HOLDING and is not used for any commercial or marketing purposes.

Security & Retention

All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We retain your information only as long as necessary to respond to your request or as required by law. Once processed, data is either deleted or archived securely.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data (Name, Email, Phone). To do so, please email us at reservation@estrellaholding.art with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Your request will be processed within 7 business days. You can also visit our Data Deletion Page for more details.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time. For any such requests, please contact us at reservation@estrellaholding.art.

Security Practices

The app transmits all user data over secure HTTPS connections. We apply organizational and technical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

Updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect legal changes or new app features. The latest version will always be accessible on this page, with the date of the last update indicated.
