Privacy Policy — Estrella Holding

Last updated: September 2025 • This policy explains how the App operates and how your information is handled.

1) Introduction

Welcome to the Estrella Holding mobile application (the “App”). We respect your privacy and design our software to minimize data collection. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2) Overview

The App provides access to property listings, company services, mortgage tools, and news — all fully offline.

  • No sign-in or account creation
  • No analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs
  • No network calls; the App does not request the INTERNET permission

3) Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal data with our servers or third parties.

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone) are sent to us
  • No device or usage data is transmitted
  • No location, camera, or files access

4) Information You May Provide Locally

You may optionally enter details in the contact form (e.g., name, email, phone, subject, message) or mortgage values. These inputs are processed only on your device to create a local PDF/CSV or to prefill a message.

Nothing is uploaded automatically.

5) User-Initiated Sharing

If you choose to contact us, the App opens your preferred communication app (Email, WhatsApp, or Phone). Sharing is initiated and confirmed by you and handled by those external apps, not by Estrella Holding.

6) Local Storage & Retention

Any files you create (PDF/CSV) remain on your device. You may delete them at any time. We do not keep copies on remote servers.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. Because we do not collect data, no personal information is obtained from any user.

8) Security

Since the App does not transmit data online, there is no remote collection. Local information remains protected by your device’s security (PIN, password, fingerprint).

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy if functionality changes (for example, adding online features). The latest version will be published in the App and on our website.
