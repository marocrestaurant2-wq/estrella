Personal Data Deletion

This page applies to the Estrella Holding application.

In accordance with our Privacy Policy, you may request the deletion of the personal data you have provided through the app, including:

  • Email address (required in the contact form)
  • Phone number (optional)

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to: reservation@estrellaholding.art with your full name, email address, and the subject line "Request for deletion of my personal data".

Processing Time

Your request will be processed within a maximum of 7 business days. Once the deletion is completed, you will receive a confirmation email.

Data Retention

No personal data is retained beyond the time required to process your request. If you do not submit a deletion request, your information remains strictly confidential and is never shared with third parties.
